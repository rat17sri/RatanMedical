iShuttle Voice Recorder App for iPhone


Finally, a better way to dictate!

  • iShuttle is a new application from Advance Transcription that turns your iPhone into the best voice recorder and dictation system on the planet

  • iShuttle application is full featured and allows the user to record any number of voice dictations and store them on your iPhone

  • When ready, with a single click, you can upload them wirelessly using either a Wi-Fi or cellular connection to the Advance Transcription server for transcription by your existing transcriptionis

  • All data transfer between your iPhone and the iShuttle web server is handled in a secure, HIPAA compliant fashion.

  • iShuttle application is free to download and use for voice recording for anyone.
